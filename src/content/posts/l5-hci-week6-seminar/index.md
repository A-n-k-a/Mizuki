## Exercise 2 ‚Äì Fitts‚Äô Law
Recall from lectures that Fitts‚Äô Law is
$$
T = 100 \log_2 (D/W + 1)
$$
where D is the distance from the mouse pointer to the target, W is the width of the target, and T is the time to reach the target.
